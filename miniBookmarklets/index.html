Paste my email

This script types your email (or any text you often need to type) in the currently focused element. 

javascript:(function(d){d=document.activeElement;if(d.tagName=="INPUT"||d.tagName=="TEXTAREA"||d.contentEditable=="true"){d.value=d.innerHTML="JOHN@DOE.COM"}})()

=> My email (Drag it to your bookmarks)
